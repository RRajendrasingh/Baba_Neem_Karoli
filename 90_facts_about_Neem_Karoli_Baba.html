90 facts about Neem Karoli Baba.

  1. Neem Karoli Baba, also known as Neeb Karori Baba, was a renowned Indian spiritual guru.
  2. He was born as Lakshmi Narayan Sharma on September 11, 1900, in Akbarpur, Uttar Pradesh, India.
  3. As a child, he displayed spiritual inclinations and was attracted to sadhus (holy men) and sages.
  4. Neem Karoli Baba left home at a young age in search of spiritual wisdom and wandered across India.
  5. He underwent intense spiritual practices and gained profound insights into various religious traditions.
  6. Neem Karoli Baba was known for his simplicity and humility, often wearing a simple blanket and a dhoti (traditional Indian garment).
  7. He had a remarkable ability to connect with people from all walks of life, regardless of their social or religious background.
  8. One of his most famous disciples is Ram Dass (formerly Richard Alpert), an American psychologist and spiritual teacher.
  9. Despite being an illiterate, Neem Karoli Baba possessed immense wisdom and knowledge.
  10. He emphasized the importance of selfless service and encouraged his followers to help others without any expectations.
  11. Neem Karoli Baba often performed miracles, such as materializing objects and healing the sick.
  12. He had a deep love for Hanuman, the monkey deity in Hindu mythology, and Hanuman became an important symbol in his teachings.
  13. Neem Karoli Baba's ashrams, or spiritual centers, are spread across India and have become places of pilgrimage for spiritual seekers.
  14. He believed in the unity of all religions and taught that all paths lead to the same ultimate truth.
  15. Neem Karoli Baba was known for his sense of humor and often used jokes and anecdotes to convey spiritual teachings.
  16. Many stories and legends surround Neem Karoli Baba's life, depicting his extraordinary powers and compassion.
  17. He had a profound impact on Western seekers who traveled to India in search of spiritual guidance during the 1960s and 1970s.
  18. Neem Karoli Baba's teachings were characterized by love, devotion, and surrender to the divine.
  19. He stressed the importance of developing unconditional love towards all beings, seeing the divine in everyone.
  20. Neem Karoli Baba was known for his ability to accurately predict future events and provide guidance to his disciples.
  21. He advocated for environmental conservation and urged his followers to respect and protect nature.
  22. Neem Karoli Baba had a significant influence on the founding of the famous Kainchi Dham ashram in Uttarakhand, India.
  23. Despite his fame, he remained detached from worldly possessions and maintained a renunciate lifestyle.
  24. Neem Karoli Baba's spiritual practices included chanting, meditation, and devotional singing.
  25. He had a magnetic personality and radiated an aura of love and compassion wherever he went.
  26. Neem Karoli Baba often emphasized the importance of cultivating a personal relationship with God or the divine.
  27. He believed in the power of faith and encouraged his followers to surrender their worries and trust in a higher power.
  28. Neem Karoli Baba's teachings were not limited to any particular religion or dogma but encompassed universal spiritual principles.
